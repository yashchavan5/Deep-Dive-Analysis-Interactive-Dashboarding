KPI Definitions & Report

Formal definitions, formulas, and business rationale

1. Conversion Rate (CVR)
CVR = (Paid Customers / Total Visitors) × 100

Measures acquisition funnel effectiveness. A 1pp improvement at 48K monthly visitors = ~480 additional paying customers. SaaS benchmark: 3–5%; our target ≥7%.

2. Customer Churn Rate
Churn = (Lost Customers / Start-of-Period) × 100

At 2.3% monthly churn, ~27% of customers leave annually. Reducing by 0.5pp adds ~$280K in retained revenue. Target: below 2% by Q2 2025.

3. Average Order Value (AOV)
AOV = Total Revenue / Number of Orders

Proxy for upsell effectiveness. A $50 AOV increase across 17K orders = $850K incremental annual revenue without new customers.

4. Customer Lifetime Value (LTV)
LTV = AOV × Purchase Frequency × Avg. Lifespan

Determines CAC ceiling. LTV:CAC should be ≥3:1. Current $1,840 LTV with $380 CAC gives a healthy 4.8:1 ratio. Downtrend warrants monitoring.

5. Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR)
MRR = Σ(Active Subscriptions × Monthly Plan Price)

Core SaaS health metric. Net New MRR = New + Expansion − Churned − Contraction. Our Net New MRR of $142K/mo vs $380K Gross New MRR means churn consumes 37% of new revenue — a key H1 2025 optimization target.
